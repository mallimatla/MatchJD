POWER PURCHASE AGREEMENT

This POWER PURCHASE AGREEMENT ("Agreement") is entered into as of February 1, 2026

BETWEEN:

SELLER: Midwest Solar Farm LLC
Address: 100 Renewable Drive, Springfield, IL 62701
("Seller")

AND

BUYER: Central Illinois Electric Cooperative
Address: 500 Utility Boulevard, Decatur, IL 62521
("Buyer")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Seller is developing a solar photovoltaic generating facility located in Sangamon County, Illinois; and

WHEREAS, Buyer desires to purchase the electrical output from the Facility.

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1 - FACILITY DESCRIPTION

1.1 Facility Name: Springfield Solar Project
1.2 Location: Sangamon County, Illinois
1.3 Contract Capacity: 100 MW AC
1.4 Expected Annual Generation: 175,000 MWh

ARTICLE 2 - TERM

2.1 Contract Term: Twenty (20) years from the Commercial Operation Date
2.2 Expected Commercial Operation Date: January 1, 2028
2.3 Guaranteed Commercial Operation Date: No later than June 30, 2028

ARTICLE 3 - PRICE AND PAYMENT

3.1 Contract Price: Buyer shall pay Seller Thirty-Five Dollars ($35.00) per MWh for all energy delivered to the Delivery Point.

3.2 Annual Escalation: The Contract Price shall escalate by one and one-half percent (1.5%) per year on each anniversary of the Commercial Operation Date.

3.3 Year 1 Price: $35.00/MWh
3.4 Year 10 Price (estimated): $40.57/MWh
3.5 Year 20 Price (estimated): $47.04/MWh

3.4 Payment Terms: Buyer shall pay Seller within thirty (30) days of receipt of invoice.

ARTICLE 4 - DELIVERY POINT

4.1 Delivery Point: The Delivery Point shall be the high-side of the Project substation interconnected with Buyer's transmission system at the Springfield 138kV substation.

4.2 Title Transfer: Title to and risk of loss of energy shall transfer from Seller to Buyer at the Delivery Point.

ARTICLE 5 - CURTAILMENT

5.1 Economic Curtailment: Buyer may curtail deliveries when market prices fall below zero.

5.2 Curtailment Compensation: Seller shall be compensated for curtailed energy at 50% of the Contract Price for the first 5% of annual generation curtailed, and 100% of Contract Price thereafter.

5.3 Maximum Curtailment: Total curtailment shall not exceed 10% of expected annual generation.

ARTICLE 6 - PERFORMANCE GUARANTEES

6.1 Minimum Annual Generation: Seller guarantees minimum annual generation of 157,500 MWh (90% of expected generation).

6.2 Availability Guarantee: Seller guarantees facility availability of 97% during daylight hours.

6.3 Performance Damages: For each MWh below the Minimum Annual Generation, Seller shall pay Buyer the difference between the Contract Price and the market replacement cost.

ARTICLE 7 - TERMINATION

7.1 Buyer Termination Rights:
    (a) Seller fails to achieve Commercial Operation by the Guaranteed Date
    (b) Generation falls below 80% of guaranteed minimum for two consecutive years
    (c) Material breach uncured for ninety (90) days

7.2 Seller Termination Rights:
    (a) Buyer fails to make payment for sixty (60) days after due date
    (b) Material breach uncured for ninety (90) days

7.3 Termination Fee: Upon termination by Buyer for convenience (if permitted), Buyer shall pay a termination fee equal to three (3) years of expected revenue.

ARTICLE 8 - ENVIRONMENTAL ATTRIBUTES

8.1 Renewable Energy Credits: All Renewable Energy Credits (RECs) generated by the Facility shall be transferred to Buyer at no additional cost.

8.2 Carbon Credits: Any carbon credits or similar environmental attributes shall belong to Buyer.

ARTICLE 9 - INTERCONNECTION

9.1 Seller Responsibility: Seller shall be responsible for all costs associated with interconnecting the Facility to the grid.

9.2 Network Upgrades: Estimated network upgrade costs of $2.5 million shall be borne by Seller.
