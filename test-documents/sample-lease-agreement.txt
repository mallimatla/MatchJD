SOLAR LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

This SOLAR LAND LEASE AGREEMENT ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2026 ("Effective Date")

BETWEEN:

LESSOR: John Smith and Mary Smith, husband and wife
Address: 1234 Farm Road, Springfield, IL 62701
Entity Type: Individual

AND

LESSEE: SunPower Solar Development LLC
Address: 500 Solar Way, Suite 200, Chicago, IL 60601
Entity Type: Limited Liability Company

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Sangamon County, Illinois, more particularly described as:

PROPERTY DESCRIPTION:
Parcel Numbers: APN 123-456-789, APN 123-456-790
Legal Description: The Northwest Quarter of Section 12, Township 15 North, Range 5 West of the Third Principal Meridian, Sangamon County, Illinois
Total Acreage: 320 acres

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease the Property for the purpose of constructing, operating, and maintaining a solar photovoltaic energy generation facility.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1 - LEASE TERM

1.1 Initial Term: The initial term of this Lease shall be twenty-five (25) years, commencing on the Commencement Date.

1.2 Commencement Date: The Commencement Date shall be January 1, 2027, or such earlier date as Lessee begins construction.

1.3 Expiration Date: December 31, 2051

1.4 Extension Options: Lessee shall have three (3) options to extend the term for additional periods of five (5) years each, upon written notice to Lessor not less than one hundred eighty (180) days prior to the expiration of the then-current term.

ARTICLE 2 - RENT

2.1 Base Rent: Lessee shall pay to Lessor annual rent of One Thousand Two Hundred Dollars ($1,200.00) per acre, for a total annual rent of Three Hundred Eighty-Four Thousand Dollars ($384,000.00).

2.2 Annual Escalation: The Base Rent shall increase by two percent (2%) per year on each anniversary of the Commencement Date.

2.3 Signing Bonus: Upon execution of this Agreement, Lessee shall pay to Lessor a one-time signing bonus of Fifty Thousand Dollars ($50,000.00).

2.4 Payment Schedule: Rent shall be paid annually in advance on the first day of each lease year.

ARTICLE 3 - PERMITTED USES

3.1 Lessee may use the Property for the following purposes:
    (a) Construction, installation, operation, and maintenance of solar photovoltaic panels and related equipment
    (b) Construction of access roads, fencing, and security systems
    (c) Installation of electrical transmission lines and interconnection facilities
    (d) Battery energy storage systems
    (e) Any other uses reasonably necessary for a solar energy facility

ARTICLE 4 - PURCHASE OPTION

4.1 Grant of Option: Lessor hereby grants to Lessee the exclusive option to purchase the Property.

4.2 Purchase Price: The purchase price shall be determined by an independent MAI appraisal conducted within six (6) months of exercise, based on fair market value for agricultural use, excluding any value attributable to the solar improvements.

4.3 Exercise Period: The option may be exercised at any time during the Lease term upon one hundred twenty (120) days written notice.

ARTICLE 5 - TERMINATION

5.1 Lessee Termination: Lessee may terminate this Agreement upon twelve (12) months written notice if:
    (a) Required permits cannot be obtained
    (b) Interconnection agreement cannot be secured
    (c) Power purchase agreement cannot be obtained on commercially reasonable terms

5.2 Lessor Termination: Lessor may terminate this Agreement only upon material breach by Lessee that remains uncured for sixty (60) days after written notice.

ARTICLE 6 - INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

6.1 Lessee shall maintain comprehensive general liability insurance of not less than Five Million Dollars ($5,000,000) per occurrence.

6.2 Lessee shall indemnify and hold Lessor harmless from any claims arising from Lessee's use of the Property.
